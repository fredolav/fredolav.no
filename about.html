For the last decade I have been exploring the possibilities of the web in different job roles and projects.

From 2010 to 2015 I worked as a web journalist at NRK (Norwegian Broadcasting Corporation), creating a broad range of web content including text, audio, video and photography, my main focus being on writing.

Since 2015 I have been the web editor of the Oslo Philharmonic. In this role I have been in charge of planning and implementing several web projects, including the company website. I have also written biographies and program notes for the orchestra.

For the last few years I have been delving into programming in my spare time, to be able to create web sites and projects from scratch.
