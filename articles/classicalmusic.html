CLASSICAL MUSIC

Classical music has been the central focus of my career.

My first job was behind the counter of a classical music CD shop in the 1990s. From 2006 to 2015 I worked as a journalist at the national broadcaster NRK.

Since 2015 I've been the web editor of the Oslo Philharmonic. Today I write most of the program notes for the concerts, and I regularly do pre-concert talks.
